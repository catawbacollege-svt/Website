Our story

We are creating a hands-on engineering program through solar racing.

Founded in 2025, the team brings together students who want to learn by building. Our first major milestone was acquiring a solar car from Appalachian State University, and we are now developing it for competition while expanding our skills in design, fabrication, systems integration, and project management.
