About us

A student-led solar racing team built on curiosity and momentum.

Founded in 2025 at Catawba College in Salisbury, NC, our team exists to give students a real engineering environment where ideas become hardware. We are taking our first solar car from Appalachian State University and developing it into a competition-ready vehicle.

Contact the Team

Learning by building

We use the car as a platform for education, outreach, and competition.

Our mission

More than a race car.

The team is a hands-on learning experience that connects engineering, leadership, and community engagement.

1

Build technical confidence

Students get practice with systems thinking, troubleshooting, fabrication, and design decisions.

2

Strengthen leadership

Members organize meetings, manage deadlines, communicate with sponsors, and keep the project moving.

3

Support sustainability

Solar racing demonstrates what efficient, clean, and creative transportation can look like.

4

Represent Catawba well

We aim to bring pride to the college through public-facing work and competition readiness.
